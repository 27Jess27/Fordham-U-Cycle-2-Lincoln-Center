How Educating a Woman Can Effect A World

Educating a women can create positive and beneficical changes to a society. Such benefits include:

  • 1. Empowering women to be leaders not only in the household, but in the workplace.
  • 2. Helping to improve survival rate
  • 3. Protecting children's rights
  • 4. Reducing child marriage
