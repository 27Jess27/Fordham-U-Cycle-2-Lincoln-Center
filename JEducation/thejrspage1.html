What's the Problem?

Millions of girls are being denied access to education all around the world. In specific locations such as the ones listed, there are millions of girls out of school.

  • 1. Nigeria- almost five and a half million
  • 2. Pakistan- over three million
  • 3. Ethiopia- over one million

Girls with higher levels of education are less likely to get married at an early age. If all women had a secondary education, child deaths would be cut in half, saving three million lives.
